Food For a Family

The Red Cross Society of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, supported by the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies, has so far distributed 288 metric tons of yellow maize to 6,051 of the most vulnerable families (24,827 people).

Shelter For a Family

The community flood shelter is a large earthen mound designated to function as a shelter in the first place. It can accommodate families within the range of 150–250 (found from the survey). In community shelters, people usually stay in makeshift houses.

Education For a Child

Children from poor families are the least likely to attend early childhood education programmes. For children who do have access, poorly trained educators, overcrowded and unstimulating environments, and unsuitable curricula diminish the quality of their experiences.
